How does it work?

Wakanow Pay Small Small (PSS) is a flexible travel payment plan that allows customers lock down great travel deals by making as little as 25% down payment of the total travel cost and paying the rest in convenient instalments. PSS allows you to save as much as 150% by taking advantage of the ‘early bird’ fares, and spread your payment over a convenient period of time.

Pay Small Small is open to all Wakanow Customers

  1. All installments must be paid in full before the travel date, according to the selected payment options.
  2. All transactions made on the “Pay Small Small” scheme are subject to a minimum 25% down payment and service charges. (payable upfront and non-refundable)
  3. Subsequent installments will be made via direct debit on the same card for down payments made with the card. This means that Wakanow has the authority to subsequently debit the card-linked account automatically for the balance after the initial deposit.
  4. 4. Flight tickets and Hotel reservations will be locked down upon initial down payment. A lien will be placed on the ticket until all installments are paid in full.
  5. Date Change is permitted. Customers will be required to pay the difference in fare and applicable charges.
  6. However, Changes would only be effected when full PSS payments have been completed.
  7. Customer is prohibited from utilizing hotel reservations until all installments are paid in full.
  8. Default Policy
    • Default on the agreed payment plan will attract a 5% penalty charge
    • In case of default and expiration of loan tenor, a Tenor modification rule (Payment date would be moved to the closest applicable tenor which cannot exceed original travel date where new tenor rates would be applicable) would apply
    • Ticket will be canceled 3 days to the travel date or after a 30day grace period (whichever is closer)
  9. Refund policy:
    • For a refundable ticket, the down payment will be utilized to pay all cancellation fees for the flight or hotel.

      However, if the cancellation fees exceed the down payment, the relevant deductions will be made from the amount paid before refund.

    • For a non-refundable ticket, the customer shall not be entitled to any refund in the event of cancellation.
  10. Travel Insurance policy on Pay Small Small is not confirmed until final payment.
  11. Tickets will not be released BEFORE payment of the final installment to procure Traveler’s Allowance (PTA or BTA)
